CHET
BAKER

A jazz trumpet runner and singer in the United States.
Famous for his loose, romantic, and somewhat melancholy
-feeling trumpet performance and singing, as well as
his tremendous crazy life reversal. Master of West Coast
jazz and one of the legendary musicians representing Cool
jazz. His real name is Chesney Henry Baker, Jr. (Chesney
Henry Baker, Jr.) Born on December 23, 1929 and died on
May 13, 1988.

All the young musician Chet Baker loved his music, but
only his lovers Jane and Trumpet remained beside him the
moment they could no longer play. There is music that I
want to play even if this is the last moment, and there
is a life I want to live. Again, in Chet Baker's own way.
